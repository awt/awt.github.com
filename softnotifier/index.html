SoftNotifier

SoftNotifier saves your productivity.

See the little black dot up there? That’s SoftNotifier. It lets you know you’ve got mail in the most unobtrusive way possible, so you can stay focused on the task at hand.

How it works

SoftNotifier uses a technique called gradual notification to imperceptibly darken as you receive more and more email.
